Azicon-500 1

Description:

White coloured, elongated, biconvex film coated tablets packed in Alu-Alu strips, enclosed in carton box.

Composition:

Each film coated tablet contains:
Azicon-500 Alu-Alu strips, enclosed in carton box.

Mechanism of Action:

Nimesulide is a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID). It works by blocking the release of certain chemical messengers that cause pain and inflammation (redness and swelling).

Ambroxol Hydrochloride is a Mucolytic medication. It thins and loosens mucus (phlegm) in the nose, windpipe and lungs making it easier to cough out.

Cetirizine Hydrochloride a second generation (Non-sedating) antihistaminic drug which antagonizes the H1 receptors. It treats allergy symptoms such as itching, swelling, and rashes by blocking the effects of a chemical messenger (histamine) in the body.

Phenylephrine Hydrochloride is a decongestant. It works by narrowing the small blood vessels which provides temporary relief from inflammation (redness and swelling) and discomfort.

Indication:

Primarily used to treat fever, body pain, severe cold/allergic conditions, runny nose and nasal congestion.

White coloured, elongated, biconvex film coated tablets packed in Alu-Alu strips, enclosed in carton box.

Composition:

Each film coated tablet contains:
Azicon-500 Alu-Alu strips, enclosed in carton box.

Mechanism of Action:

Nimesulide is a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID). It works by blocking the release of certain chemical messengers that cause pain and inflammation (redness and swelling).

Ambroxol Hydrochloride is a Mucolytic medication. It thins and loosens mucus (phlegm) in the nose, windpipe and lungs making it easier to cough out.

Cetirizine Hydrochloride a second generation (Non-sedating) antihistaminic drug which antagonizes the H1 receptors. It treats allergy symptoms such as itching, swelling, and rashes by blocking the effects of a chemical messenger (histamine) in the body.

Phenylephrine Hydrochloride is a decongestant. It works by narrowing the small blood vessels which provides temporary relief from inflammation (redness and swelling) and discomfort.

Indication:

Primarily used to treat fever, body pain, severe cold/allergic conditions, runny nose and nasal congestion.
